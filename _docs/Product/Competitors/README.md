Competitors Research
====================

This folder contains competitive analysis and research on companies building similar or adjacent products to Agipo.

## Structure

Each competitor has their own folder with detailed analysis:

- **Asteroid/** - Browser automation and AI agent platform
  - Technology stack analysis
  - Architecture insights
  - Product feature breakdown
  - Key learnings and takeaways

## Purpose

Understanding competitor technologies, architectures, and approaches helps us:
- Identify proven patterns we should consider
- Find gaps or opportunities in their approach
- Learn from their mistakes and successes
- Inform our own technical and product decisions
