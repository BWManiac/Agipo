What This Template Does

The Smart Email Processor automatically analyzes incoming emails using advanced AI to extract meaningful information and automate common tasks. Perfect for busy professionals who want to streamline their email workflow.

Key Features

  • Intelligent Categorization: Automatically sorts emails into predefined categories based on content analysis
  • Entity Extraction: Identifies important information like dates, names, amounts, and contact details
  • Priority Scoring: Assigns priority levels based on sender importance, urgency indicators, and content analysis
  • Task Creation: Automatically creates actionable tasks in your preferred project management tool
  • Smart Notifications: Sends intelligent alerts for high-priority emails requiring immediate attention

How It Works

  1. Email Ingestion: Connects to your Gmail account and monitors for new emails
  2. Content Analysis: Uses GPT-4 to analyze email content, subject, and metadata
  3. Information Extraction: Identifies key entities, sentiment, and action items
  4. Task Generation: Creates structured tasks in Notion, Asana, or other tools
  5. Notification: Sends summary reports and urgent alerts via Slack or email

  • Customer service teams processing support requests

  • Project managers coordinating team communications
  • Anyone looking to achieve inbox zero efficiently
